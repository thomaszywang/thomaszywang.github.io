About

I am a Master's graduate from the State Key Laboratory of Optoelectronic Materials and Technologies at Sun Yat-sen University, where I was privileged to be advised by Prof. Zong Qin. I am currently applying for PhD programs in 2027.

My research integrates display technologies with image processing, with a focus on the development and practical application of Field Sequential Color (FSC) driving algorithms. I have published in Optics Express and Optics Letters, gave an oral presentation at the SID conference, and have had the opportunity to collaborate on projects with companies such as CSOT, Huawei, and Hisense.

Outside of my academic pursuits, I am passionate about traveling and photography. More of my work can be found in the photography section.

Projects

Design of VR Optical Machine Based on FSC Display
Jan 2023 – Dec 2023
Project Leader · In collaboration with China Star Optoelectronics Technology (CSOT)
  • Developed a VR optical engine prototype based on Field Sequential Color (FSC) display technology, addressing demands for high resolution, high brightness, and cost efficiency.
  • Applied multi-objective optimization and deep learning to the driving algorithm, enabling real-time high-resolution image generation with low distortion and minimal color breakup.
  • Presented the final VR prototype at the 2023 Society for Information Display (SID) International Symposium.
Multi-focal Plane AR Head-Up Display (AR-HUD)
Jun 2021 – Feb 2022
Algorithm Development Leader · In collaboration with Huawei Technologies
  • Developed an ultra-low-complexity driving algorithm for multi-focal plane AR-HUD systems based on LCoS displays in automotive environments.
  • Enabled real-time high-resolution image generation while effectively mitigating color breakup, delivering enhanced stability and brightness for automotive HUD applications.
